## Role chosen: Debate coach

## Topic: Should schools allow students to use cell phones?

### What I was trying to do
Practice live debate. I asked for the three strongest arguments on each side, then had it pick the most convincing "against" argument and rebut it. After that, I argued the "for" side myself and had it play "against" and score my rebuttal.

### What I liked
It gave me a proper argument for both sides instead of just one side, which was pretty fair.
The score feedback it gave me was very useful and gave me specifically what would push it to a higher score.
It also turned my own argument back on me. "Students get distracted by laptops and daydreaming too, so why ban only phones?"

### What I didn't like
I felt the AI was being too smart and finding arguments against me too fast. Maybe I can tell it to act like a human so that it doesn't get too smart with its arguments.
